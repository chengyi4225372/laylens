Hunter Careers

Hunter offers many exciting career opportunities in a variety of roles, including Independent Sales & Service Representatives, Corporate positions and Engineering Co-Ops.

Sales Representative

Hunter fields the largest dedicated sales force in the undercar service industry, and supports Sales Representatives through routine training, Service Representatives and Franchised Distributors.

Service Representative

Hunter Service Representatives provide installation of new equipment, warranty service for new products and continued service for the life of Hunter equipment.

Corporate Opportunities

Hunter offers exciting career opportunities in multiple fields, including Engineering, Purchasing, Customer Service and Information Technology.

Manufacturing Opportunities

Hunter's world class products wouldn’t be possible without its in-house manufacturing experts, Hunter offers a wide variety of opportunities within its manufacturing facility.

Student Co-op

Hunter is proud to offer co-op opportunities in a variety of Engineering fields, including mechanical, electrical and software engineering.
