LIFT RACKS

Industry-Leading Alignment Lift Racks

Hunter Engineering offers a full line of standard and heavy duty truck and car lift racks built to minimize space while maximizing productivity. From light duty alignment lifts built for passenger and light trucks to heavy-duty pit racks, Hunter is the leading producer of vehicle lifts.
RX16

The flush mount RX16 offers maximum capacity in a low profile, compact package. Extra wide runways, swing-air jacks, and a multitude of productivity features make this rack the most capable available.

RX14

The surface mount RX14 offers a high capacity rack for existing service bays. Extra wide runways, swing-air jacks, and a multitude of productivity features make this rack the most capable surface mount rack available.

RX12

The RX wheel alignment scissor lift series features extra-wide 24” runways, along with best-in-class drive-on height.

RX10

The Hunter RX10 car scissor lift series maximizes productivity while utilizing limited space.

FOUR-POST

Hunter’s four-post alignment lift family offers high-capacity open- and closed-front alignment lifts with extra-wide runways.

PIT RACKS

Hunter's pit rack for passenger cars and light duty trucks has wider runways than the competition, making it one of the most versatile truck and car lift racks available.

LIFT ACCESSORIES

Hunter also provides a full-line of wheel alignment lift rack accessories to enhance serviceability and ease-of-use.
