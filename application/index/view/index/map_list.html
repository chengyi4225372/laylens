St. Louis, Missouri Headquarters

Address: 11250 Hunter Drive
Bridgeton, Missouri 63044
Phone: 314-731-3020
800-448-6848
Fax: 314-731-1776

Directions:
The St. Louis corporate offices of Hunter Engineering are located at the North East corner of I-70 and Lindbergh Blvd. (Hwy 67).

Click Here to Enlarge and Print Location Map

From Eastbound I-70:
Take the Lindbergh Blvd. exit number 235B. Drive North to Natural Bridge Rd. (B) exit, turn right. Drive East on Natural Bridge Rd. Turn right onto Schuette Dr. then turn left into the parking lot. The main office building has a fountain in front of the reception area.

From Westbound I-70:
Take the Natural Bridge Rd. exit number 235C. At traffic light, turn left onto Natural Bridge Rd. Turn left onto Hunter Dr. Drive past the Training Center (right) and the fountain (left) then turn right on Schuette Dr. Turn right into the parking lot. The main office building has a fountain in front of the reception area.
