Mercedes-Benz

Mercedes Alignment solutions & more from Hunter Engineering

Hunter is proud to offer a full line of Mercedes-authorized OEM equipment designed to meet virtually any need a shop specializing in Mercedes-Benz may need. We provide Mercedes alignment solutions for both passenger & heavy duty vehicles offering the HTA-MB-TD alignment system customized exclusively for all Mercedes-Benz vehicles, with high-precision alignment features, customized alignment software and complete Mercedes-Benz model support. These systems utilize Hunter’s patented HawkEye technology, and are sensors and consoles are customizable to meet the unique needs of your Mercedes-Benz Workshop. Our customized Mercedes-Benz HD Wheel Alignment system is a true solution for shops servicing Mercedes-Benz commercial vehicles exceeding new equipment requirements for Mercedes-Benz workshops, featuring self-centering adapters, a patented truck pusher and mobile turnplates standard. The GSP97MB Road Force Touch Mercedes Wheel Balancer is designed exclusively for Mercedes-Benz workshops, featuring more speed & precision than an ordinary wheel balancer. Hunter’s revolutionary Quick Check™ Inspection System for Mercedes-Benz can be configured for any shop with dozens of installation possibilities and is designed to yield a positive return for your Mercedes-Benz workshop, identifying repair opportunities in under 3 minutes. Hunter also offers the 4PMB 4-post vehicle lift for Mercedes-Benz, featuring patented PowerSlide® control and 18,000-lb capacity. It is the only Mercedes-Benz approved alignment rack for the Sprinter, featuring a 26 in. runway width.
